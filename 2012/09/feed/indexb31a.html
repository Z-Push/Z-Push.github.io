today we have released the Z-Push 2.0.3 final version (build 1467).
It fixes various issues, especially regarding appointments and with enabled SMS sync on Android devices. Also the issues with special chars on an Android 4 and Windows Phone were fixed. Rare apache segfaults on RHEL-based systems are also fixed.
There is a new configuration parameter, FILEAS_ORDER, which will help to organise your contacts better.
We have also merged dupondje’s contributions for IMAP and combined backend, thanks to him for his work.
