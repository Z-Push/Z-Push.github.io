we have just released Z-Push 2.6.2 final (tag 2.6.2 commit b89d96ea4f4c494ddcb32f9447b9e3fcabd87b81).

This maintenance release includes some bug fixes.

The issue that Outlook calendar is stuck in sync has been resolved.

Merijn contributed a PHP 7.4 related fix.

Michael provided a couple IMAP and CardDav related contributions.

Install Z-Push 2.6.2 final from the final repositories (recommended) Z-Push Wiki.

Thanks a lot to Z-Push contributors Merijn and Michael! Your contributions are highly appreciated. Also thanks to everybody providing us feedback.
