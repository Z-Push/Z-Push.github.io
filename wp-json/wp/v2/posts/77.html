On 13\/01\/2011 we have released the final version of Z-Push 1.5.<\/p>\n

This version introduces several innovations to Z-Push.
\nNew backends were added, libraries were updated, logging was improved, PEAR dependency removed, configurable state directory, improved results from GAL search and many more. The full changelog is available\u00a0here<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Attention:<\/strong>\u00a0When upgrading from a version before 1.5 you MUST adjust the “state-directory” setting in the config.php file, or your mobile will go out of sync!
\nIf you want to make use of the possibility to store the states in a folder outside of Z-Push, make sure you copy or move the old states to the new location before connecting with a mobile.<\/p>\n

Download at:\u00a0http:\/\/z-push.sourceforge.net\/download<\/a><\/p>\n
