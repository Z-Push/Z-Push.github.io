Z-Push 1 future

by Manfred | Jun 28, 2012 | News | 0 comments

Hi all,

with the release of Z-Push 2.0 final on the 21st of June 2012 we have reached a new milestone in the Z-Push development. In this post I would like to share our view regarding Z-Push 1 and what is going to happen with it. We have ceased the development of the new features for Z-Push 1 in order to put our complete effort into Z-Push 2 development. We encourage you to migrate to Z-Push 2 already as it has improved in many aspects and the debugging of issues is also easier for us. We will merge the fixes back into Z-Push 1 where possible and also fix the issues which aren’t too complex. This complementary support will be available for about 6 months until the 31st of December 2012. Afterwards we will offer upgrade support only.

Greets, Manfred
Z-Push dev team
