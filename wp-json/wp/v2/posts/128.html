Hi all,<\/p>\n

today we have released the Z-Push 2.1.1 final version (build 1788).<\/p>\n

Important notes<\/strong><\/p>\n

There were several reports in the last weeks about broken meeting requests where the “attendee becomes the organizer”.<\/p>\n

We could limit that to the combination of Z-Push 2.0.8 and iOS 7. If you use these versions you are strongly encouraged to update.<\/p>\n

If your users see broken items in their calendars, there is a python script available to fix these. You can get this script at:<\/p>\n

http:\/\/svn.berlios.de\/wsvn\/z-push\/trunk\/tools\/fix-meetings-2.0.8%2B2.1.0-ios7.py<\/p>\n

We don’t give any support on this script. Use it at your own risk.<\/p>\n

We also had reports about several issues related with the BlackBerry 10 OS.<\/p>\n

1. if you see “?” in a senders name on the mobile you should upgrade your BB firmware to version 10.2.0.415 or higher.<\/p>\n

2. in replied or forwarded emails high characters are broken<\/p>\n

3. in some occasions there was no body displayed on the mobile for emails with attachments<\/p>\n

Issues 2 and 3 are fixed in this release.<\/p>\n

Also the issue that if meeting request was sent from a mobile device, free busy information in the organizer’s calendar was not available in Outlook or Webapp, has been fixed.<\/p>\n

Changelog<\/a>\u00a0since Z-Push 2.1.0.<\/p>\n

Your feedback is highly appreciated.<\/p>\n

Kind regards,<\/p>\n

Manfred
