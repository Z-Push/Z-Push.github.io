Invitation to the Z-Push Community Survey

by Admin | Feb 3, 2016 | News | 0 comments

Dear Z-Push users,

today instead of announcing a new release we want to invite you to a small survey.

The goal of this survey is to get a better insight on how Z-Push is used and in which environments and therefore hope to better prioritise features and releases.

This survey will take roughly five minutes and will be open until the end of February 2016.

http://goo.gl/forms/h4h9qEqgGu

Thanks for your participation and kind regards,
Sebastian
Z-Push dev team
