Hi all,<\/p>\n

after one year of intensive development we have just released Z-Push 2.3.0 final (tag 2.3.0, commit cc7ee228a5b1421268db37a0e1f7f9725ac1ffdf).<\/p>\n

There are many new features in Z-Push 2.3.0, like packages and repositories for 14 distributions, MySQL and memcache support for states, improved Outlook compatibility (KOE) and especially: a vast speed improvement!
\nWe would like to thank a lot our community contributors Etienne, Francisco, Nicolas, Ralf and Vincent who helped to make this possible!<\/p>\n

Upgrade procedure<\/strong>
\nYou can directly upgrade from any Z-Push 2.2.x.
\nAfter the upgrade, “z-push-admin -a fixstates<\/em>” needs to be executed once!
\nElse the Ping request will not block and devices will sync continuously.
\nAfter the fixstates all mobiles will work normally and Ping will be working as expected.
\nRead more about the upgrade procedure in our wiki<\/a>.<\/strong><\/p>\n

We strongly advise you to install the from the packages and don’t use the tarball (which we also publish)!<\/strong>
\nMore information about the installation from repositories and packages is available here: https:\/\/wiki.z-hub.io\/display\/ZP\/Installation<\/a>
\n<\/strong>
\nThe full change log is available here<\/a>.<\/p>\n

Your feedback is highly appreciated.<\/p>\n

Kind regards,
\nManfred
