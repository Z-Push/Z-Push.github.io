News | Z-Push https://z-push.org Open source push technology Sat, 01 Feb 2014 14:41:46 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=5.6.10 Z-Push 1.4 final released https://z-push.org/z-push-1-4-final-released/ https://z-push.org/z-push-1-4-final-released/#respond Fri, 18 Jun 2010 12:00:11 +0000 http://62.245.227.250/?p=71 On 14/06/2010 we have released the final version of Z-Push 1.4.

This version contains the remote wipe command line script. You can see the status of devices and request the remote wipe.

It also contains several fixes and changes for Mail4Exchange 3. If your M4E broke with previous version, you need to reconfigure your profile on the mobile.

Several issues with recurring Meeting Requests on the mobiles were fixed.
