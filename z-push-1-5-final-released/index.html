Z-Push 1.5 final released

by Sebastian | Jan 13, 2011 | News | 0 comments

On 13/01/2011 we have released the final version of Z-Push 1.5.

This version introduces several innovations to Z-Push.
New backends were added, libraries were updated, logging was improved, PEAR dependency removed, configurable state directory, improved results from GAL search and many more. The full changelog is available here.

Attention: When upgrading from a version before 1.5 you MUST adjust the “state-directory” setting in the config.php file, or your mobile will go out of sync!
If you want to make use of the possibility to store the states in a folder outside of Z-Push, make sure you copy or move the old states to the new location before connecting with a mobile.

Download at: http://z-push.sourceforge.net/download
