News | Z-Push https://z-push.org Open source push technology Sun, 02 Feb 2014 12:51:27 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=5.6.10 Z-Push 2.1.1 released https://z-push.org/z-push-2-1-1-released/ https://z-push.org/z-push-2-1-1-released/#comments Sat, 30 Nov 2013 13:00:50 +0000 http://62.245.227.250/?p=128 Hi all,

today we have released the Z-Push 2.1.1 final version (build 1788).

Important notes

There were several reports in the last weeks about broken meeting requests where the “attendee becomes the organizer”.

We could limit that to the combination of Z-Push 2.0.8 and iOS 7. If you use these versions you are strongly encouraged to update.

If your users see broken items in their calendars, there is a python script available to fix these. You can get this script at:

http://svn.berlios.de/wsvn/z-push/trunk/tools/fix-meetings-2.0.8%2B2.1.0-ios7.py

We don’t give any support on this script. Use it at your own risk.

We also had reports about several issues related with the BlackBerry 10 OS.

1. if you see “?” in a senders name on the mobile you should upgrade your BB firmware to version 10.2.0.415 or higher.

2. in replied or forwarded emails high characters are broken

3. in some occasions there was no body displayed on the mobile for emails with attachments

Issues 2 and 3 are fixed in this release.

Also the issue that if meeting request was sent from a mobile device, free busy information in the organizer’s calendar was not available in Outlook or Webapp, has been fixed.

Changelog since Z-Push 2.1.0.

Your feedback is highly appreciated.

Kind regards,

Manfred
Z-Push dev team
