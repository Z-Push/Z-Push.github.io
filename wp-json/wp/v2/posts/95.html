today we have released Z-Push 1.5.10 final version. It is identical to Z-Push-1.5.10RC-1246.<\/p>\n

Notes<\/strong><\/p>\n

This is a maintenance release for Z-Push 1.5.<\/p>\n

Zarafa\/ICS
\n[ZP-4] – Double quoted email addresses show invalid high character encoding on iOS
\n[ZP-61] – Inline.txt instead of inline image on Android<\/p>\n

The version is available at: http:\/\/z-push.sourceforge.net\/download<\/p>\n
